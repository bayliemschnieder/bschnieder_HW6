Backstory

Hi I'm Baylie Schnieder, I am currently studying business analytics and information systems at the University of Iowa. I am from Johnston, Iowa a suburb of Des Moines, Iowa. I've lived here my whole life basically, except I was born in Silvis, Illinois and lived there when I was baby. Most of my family is from Silvis or as many would say the greater Quad Cities area. So I often go there for holidays, birthday and trips visiting my family.

I have one dog, one brother, and two nephews and nieces. Over the summer's I live with my parents and spend time often with them. Family is super important to me. I've played a lot of sports in my life and it ruled a big majority of my childhood. I used to play soccer all my life until I grew out of love for the sports after my freshmen of highschool. I still played it in highschool but it wasn't the same as it used to be for me. I also played volleyball, bowling and did the marching band where I did colorguard. I also did the band playing the clarinet during the off season of marching band.
